Privacy Policy

This Data Protection Notice (“Notice”) sets out the basis on which Mindsphere Singapore Pte. Ltd. (“we”, “us”, or “our”) may collect, use, disclose, or otherwise process personal data of our customers in accordance with the Personal Data Protection Act (“PDPA”). This Notice applies to personal data in our possession or under our control, including data in possession of organisations engaged by us for data collection, usage, and processing.

Personal Data

  1. Definition: “customer” refers to an individual who either (a) contacts us regarding our goods or services, or (b) has a contractual relationship with us for goods or services.
  2. Examples of personal data we may collect include: name, address, email, telephone, nationality, gender, and date of birth.
  3. Terms not defined in this Notice shall follow the definitions provided in the PDPA.

Collection, Use, and Disclosure of Personal Data

  1. Personal data is generally collected when voluntarily provided by you or your authorised representative, or as permitted by the PDPA.
  2. We may collect and use your data for purposes such as:
    • Fulfilling obligations related to goods and services provided;
    • Verifying identity, managing interactions, and processing payments;
    • Compliance with legal obligations and responding to requests from authorities.
  3. Personal data may be disclosed as necessary for the above purposes, including to third-party service providers.
  4. Even if your relationship with us ends, we may retain your data as necessary to enforce contractual rights.

Reliance on Legitimate Interests Exception

  1. Under PDPA's legitimate interests exception, we may process personal data to prevent misuse of services, perform network analysis, and prevent fraud.
  2. These purposes may apply even if your relationship with us has changed or ended.

Withdrawing Your Consent

  1. Your consent remains valid until withdrawn. Requests to withdraw consent should be submitted in writing to our Data Protection Officer (DPO).
  2. Upon receipt, we will process your request within ten (10) business days and notify you of any consequences.
  3. If your request affects service provision, we will inform you before finalizing the withdrawal.

Access to and Correction of Personal Data

  1. Requests for access or correction of data can be submitted to our DPO. Reasonable fees may apply for access requests.
  2. We aim to respond within thirty (30) days, notifying you if additional time is needed. If unable to fulfill a request, we will generally explain why.

Protection of Personal Data

  1. We implement measures to safeguard your data from unauthorized access and other risks, including encryption, secure storage, and restricted access.
  2. While no system is completely secure, we continuously review and enhance our data protection measures.

Accuracy and Retention of Personal Data

  1. To ensure data accuracy, we rely on you to provide current, complete, and accurate information.
  2. Data is retained as long as necessary for the original purpose or as legally required. Data is securely disposed of when no longer needed.

Transfers of Data Outside Singapore

  1. We generally do not transfer data outside Singapore. If necessary, we will obtain consent and ensure data protection comparable to PDPA standards.

Data Protection Officer

Contact our Data Protection Officer for inquiries or requests at: privacy@mindsphere.sg

Effect of Notice and Updates

  1. This Notice complements other policies and contracts regarding data processing by us.
  2. We may revise this Notice periodically. Your continued use of our services indicates your acceptance of these updates.
